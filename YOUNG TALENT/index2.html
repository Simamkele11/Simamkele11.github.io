Courses

Welding

Our welding program covers a wide range of techniques, from basic to advanced welding processes. Students gain practical experience with various welding equipment and materials, learning to produce high-quality, precise welds that meet industry standards

Boiler Making

The boiler making program offers comprehensive training in the design, construction, and maintenance of boilers. Students develop a deep understanding of boiler systems, safety protocols, and the technical skills required to handle complex projects.

Carpentry

Our carpentry program emphasizes both traditional and modern woodworking techniques. Students learn to work with different materials and tools, creating everything from fine cabinetry to structural frameworks.
