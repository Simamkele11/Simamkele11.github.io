Boiler Making

Welcome to the Comprehensive Boilermaking Training course, where craftsmanship meets opportunity. This dynamic program is tailored for individuals eager to delve into the boilermaking trade, offering hands-on training and the unique advantage of getting paid for real-world projects. Our course is designed to equip you with advanced boilermaking skills while providing financial rewards through practical project work.

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced Boilermaking Techniques: Gain expertise in fabricating, assembling, and installing boilers and other heavy metal structures. Learn to work with various materials, including steel, stainless steel, and alloys
  • Blueprint and Technical Drawing Interpretation: Develop the ability to read and understand complex blueprints, schematics, and technical drawings to ensure precision and quality in your work.
  • Welding and Cutting Skills: Master essential welding and cutting techniques, including MIG, TIG, and Stick welding, tailored specifically for boilermaking tasks..
  • Safety Practices: Learn and apply stringent safety protocols to protect yourself and others, including proper use of personal protective equipment (PPE) and compliance with industry standards

Practical Training

  • Hands-On Projects: Engage in a variety of hands-on projects, from small-scale repairs to large, intricate boiler systems. Each project is designed to simulate real-world challenges and prepare you for the industry.
  • Paid Project Work: Benefit from the opportunity to earn money while learning. Students receive compensation for their contributions to various projects, providing practical experience and financial support
  • Industry Collaboration: Work alongside industry professionals and local businesses to complete projects that meet real-world specifications and standards.

Who Should Enroll
