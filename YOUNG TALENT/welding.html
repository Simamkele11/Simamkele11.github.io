Welding

Welcome to the Welding Techniques course, where industry meets education. This unique program is designed for individuals passionate about welding who want to gain hands-on experience while earning money. Our comprehensive training integrates practical skill development with real-world project work, giving you both the expertise and the financial rewards you need to jumpstart your welding career

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced Welding Skills: Master complex welding techniques including MIG, TIG, Stick, and Flux-Cored welding. Learn to work with various metals such as steel, aluminum, and stainless steel.
  • Blueprint Reading and Interpretation: Gain the ability to read and interpret detailed blueprints and technical drawings to execute precise welding projects.
  • Safety Protocols: Understand and implement essential safety measures to ensure a safe working environment, including proper use of personal protective equipment (PPE) and adherence to OSHA standards.
  • Equipment Handling: Get hands-on experience with state-of-the-art welding equipment and tools, learning their proper operation and maintenance.

Practical Training

  • Project-Based Learning: Apply your skills to real-world projects. From small repairs to large-scale constructions, you'll work on tasks that mirror the challenges faced in the industry.
  • Paid Projects: Benefit from a unique opportunity to earn while you learn. Students are compensated for their work on various projects, providing financial support and valuable industry experience.
  • Industry Partnerships: Work closely with local businesses and organizations to complete projects that address real needs and standards in the welding field

Who Should Enroll

This course is ideal for individuals seeking to build a career in welding with hands-on experience and financial compensation. Those interested in entering the welding field with practical training and earning.
